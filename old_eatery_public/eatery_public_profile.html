BB's Beach Break is a casual café with restaurant-quality dining. Located opposite North Steyne Surf Club, BB's sits right in the middle of Manly & Queenscliff. Whether you have just finished your early morning surf or having your evening stroll on the boardwalk this is the place to take your beach break. You can smell the fresh sea air at this dreamy joint – it drifts off the ocean into this Manly restaurant, tantalising you with thoughts of seafood even before you’ve glimpsed the menu. And once you do, you won’t be disappointed. Whether you’re after fish & chips, or the crispy-skinned salmon, or the delectable chilli prawns or the variety tapas selection or the seafood platter, there are always plenty of fruits of the sea on offer to help you make the most of your beachside location.
